Research Interests

  • Spectroscopy in correlated systems: ARPES, RIXS, STM
  • Quantum many-body geometry and transport properties
  • Quantum magnetism, altermagnetism, and spin liquids
  • Development of computational methods for many-body systems
  • Fock space fragmentation
  • Non-equilibrium dynamics in many-body systems

Current Projects

1. Strongly Correlated Excitations in RIXS

Studying how multi-particle excitations and fractionalization features emerge in resonant inelastic x-ray scattering spectra in quantum magnets and frustrated systems.

2. Many-Body Localization and Hilbert Space Structure

Exploring Hilbert space connectivity, effective fragmentation, and dynamical signatures in constrained interacting systems.
